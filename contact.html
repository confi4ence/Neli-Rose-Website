Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

The admission process begins with an initial enquiry and a tour of our facilities. We then conduct a comprehensive care needs assessment to determine the most appropriate level of care. Our admissions team will guide you through all the necessary paperwork and ensure a smooth, supportive transition for your loved one.

We welcome family visits during our visiting hours (Monday to Friday 09:00 – 18:00, Saturday and Sunday 10:00 – 16:00). We understand the importance of family connection and work to accommodate special requests outside these hours. During certain health circumstances, visiting protocols may be adjusted in the best interests of all residents.

We accept private payments, medical aid contributions, and can assist families with applications for government subsidies and the South African Social Security Agency (SASSA) grants where applicable. Our financial advisory team is happy to discuss options confidentially and help you find the best solution for your family.

Care plans are reviewed regularly and updated as residents' needs evolve. Our multidisciplinary team continuously monitors each resident's health and wellbeing, and we proactively communicate any changes with families. We can adjust care levels within our facility or, if required, assist with transitions to more specialised care environments.
