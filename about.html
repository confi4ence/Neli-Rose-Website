Who We Are

A Legacy of Care and Compassion

Neli-Rose Retirement Home was founded with a single, unwavering vision, to create a safe and nurturing environment where elderly individuals are treated with the respect, love, and dignity they deserve.

Established in 2015, our home has grown from a small family operation into a trusted institution serving over 100 residents and their families. Yet throughout this growth, we have never lost sight of what makes us special: the genuine, heartfelt care we provide every single day.

Our name, Neli-Rose, is a symbol of beauty, resilience, and grace, qualities we see in each and every one of our residents. Like a rose, the elderly deserve to be nurtured, appreciated, and allowed to bloom in their own time and their own way.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, person-centred care that enhances the quality of life for every resident, honouring their individuality, preserving their dignity, and supporting their families through every stage of the ageing journey.

Our Vision

To be a big retirement home in South Africa, recognised for outstanding care, innovation in senior wellbeing, and an unwavering commitment to the happiness of those entrusted to our care.

  • Family Partnership
Our Founder

The Heart Behind Neli-Rose

Beverly Kwonglee and Rodwell Kwonglee

Founders & Directors, Neli-Rose Retirement Home
"Every person who walks through our doors carries a lifetime of stories, wisdom, and love. Our responsibility is to honour that, to make sure their later years are lived with the same joy and dignity they deserve."

Our founders established Neli-Rose Retirement Home with one clear purpose: to create a space where ageing is embraced with dignity, warmth, and genuine compassion. Their vision continues to guide everything we do at Neli-Rose every single day.
