Welcome to Neli-Rose

A Home Built on Compassion and Dignity

At Neli-Rose Retirement Home, we believe that ageing is a beautiful journey, one that deserves to be celebrated with grace, warmth, and the highest standard of care. Our facility provides a safe, nurturing environment where residents are treated with the respect and love they deserve.

Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals, caregivers, and support staff are committed to enhancing the quality of life for every individual in our care, while ensuring peace of mind for their families.
